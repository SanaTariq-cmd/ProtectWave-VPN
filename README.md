## 1. Product Overview
ProtectWave VPN is a beginner-friendly VPN service designed to provide users with secure, private, and reliable internet access.  
The app focuses on simplicity and essential VPN functionality, ensuring users can protect their online privacy without complexity.

## 2. Problem Statement
Many people worry about online privacy, especially on public Wi-Fi networks or untrusted websites.  
ProtectWave VPN solves this problem by encrypting user traffic and providing a secure connection to the internet.

## 3. Target Users
- Students  
- Professionals  
- Remote workers  
- Anyone who wants private and secure internet access
